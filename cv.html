Professional Profile

An evolutionary biologist and computational geneticist with expertise in temporal genomics, hybridization genetics, and plant adaptation to climate change. Proven track record in whole-genome sequencing, bioinformatics pipelines, and interdisciplinary research, evidenced by 15+ publications and awards. Passionate about bridging science and society through biodiversity communication.

Personal Information

  • Nationality: German
  • Languages: English (Fluent); German (Proficient); Urdu (Native)

Computational and Technical Skills

Programming & Data Science

  • Languages: Python, R, Bash, Perl
  • Data Analysis & Visualization: R (tidyverse, ggplot2), Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn), Jupyter
  • Statistical Modeling: Generalized Linear/Additive Models (GLM/GAM), Bayesian Inference, Multivariate Statistics
  • Machine Learning & HPC: Proficient with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow) and high-performance computing (HPC) environments (e.g., Slurm)

Bioinformatics & Genomics

  • Genome Assembly & Annotation: De novo hybrid genome assembly (Illumina, ONT, PacBio HiFi) and scaffolding (Hi-C)
  • Temporal Population Genomics: Analysis of allele frequency change over time to detect signatures of selection (purifying and positive) and infer demographic history
  • Hybridization Genomics: Analysis of natural and experimental hybrid populations to investigate introgression, selection against hybrids, and barriers to gene flow

Population Genetics & Phylogenetics Software

  • Variant Calling & Filtering: GATK, ANGSD, BCFtools/SAMtools, Stacks, PLINK
  • Population Structure & Demography: ADMIXTURE, STRUCTURE, fastsimcoal2, ∂a∂i, TreeMix, ABBA-BABA (D-statistics), DIYABC
  • Phylogenetics & Phylogenomics: RAxML, MrBayes, BEAST, SplitsTree

Geospatial Analysis (GIS)

  • Landscape Genomics: Spatial analysis and visualization of genetic and ecological data using ArcGIS & QGIS
  • Species Distribution Modeling (SDM): Ensemble modeling using multiple algorithms (MaxEnt, GLM, GAM, Random Forest, Boosted Regression Trees)

Wet-lab Skills

  • Molecular Techniques: High-throughput DNA/RNA extraction, PCR, qPCR, gel electrophoresis, Sanger sequencing
  • Genomic Library Preparation: ddRAD-seq, Oxford Nanopore (DNA/RNA), Illumina library prep
  • Microbiology: Aseptic technique for the isolation, culturing, and maintenance of bacteria and fungi for plant microbiome analysis and pathogen assays.
